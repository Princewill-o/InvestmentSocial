Idea

Our research highlights that 50% of heating energy in commercial buildings can be wasted by empty rooms. An estimated 220k UK buildings including universities, hotels & offices are lagging behind carbon reduction targets. Rising energy costs, tightening emissions regulations and hybrid working are increasing the urgency of the problem.

At EcoSync we’re delivering a possible 30-50% reduction in heating-related carbon emissions & costs for tenants and owners.

We utilise energy-harvesting IoT tech to easily retrofit to radiators making them intelligent, only heating rooms according to occupancy & need. Once fitted, our AI-enabled software allows remote and individualised control of heating using a cloud-based dashboard & QR code app.

Our solution can be rapidly deployed with no interruption and complements other efficiency measures such as heat pumps and insulation. Our SaaS model aimed at B2B clients is easy to understand & designed for fast payback within 3 years.

Since launching in 2020, we've focused on the education sector and onboarded 11 clients, including Cambridge & Oxford Universities. We're now expanding into other sectors with expressions of interest from the MoD & Oxfordshire CC. We’ve won numerous awards, secured impact investors & have patents pending for the system.

This raise will be used to grow our AI, Sales, Marketing & Customer Success teams to accelerate growth.
