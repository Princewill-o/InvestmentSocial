Idea

The Spanish public health sector is broken, with up to 5 months of surgery waiting lists in Spain alone and patients struggle to find the appropriate treatment for their needs.

Launched in 2018, we're on a mission to resolve patient's medical needs & find the best-suited treatment plans. Through proprietary software, we provide a marketplace where patients can find the appropriate doctor, free of charge & within 24h. Health professionals access the platform via a monthly subscription fee.

After raising 1.1M€, a new platform was launched in late 2021, we've had over 8k+ patient enquiries since 2018 & onboarding 150+ paying healthcare professionals, including leading health institutions: Quirónsalud, Instituto Cugat & IMED. The number of paying users is growing at +40%/month to 150 in March '22. 

We started by focusing on Spain, a market worth c.€33BN annually at a CAGR of c.3.2%, targeting the high percentage of international patients, growing at 20% globally. We now aim to expand internationally, as the persistent shortages of health provision services in several European countries provide an opportunity to grow and provide a digital customer experience.

Funds raised will be targeted to further grow software functionalities, broaden sales & marketing, expand internationally and boost patient onboarding strategies.
