Idea

Despite rising prevalence in limited edition sneaker buying, our experience shows buyers have no easy means to access this stock - incumbent channels include Instagram, eBay & StockX, where product authenticity and quick access is challenging.

Founder, Moses, established The Edit LDN to connect investors and owners of sneakers with buyers, redefining this market. Stock is sold through The Edit Ldn's ecommerce platform and a Harrods concession.

Vetted and approved sellers have access to an online inventory log & API technology through our partner, which tracks real time sneaker market prices. The Edit LDN receives a commission on sale, and all items are shipped to HQ for authentication checks before customer shipping.

Cowen estimated the global sneaker resale market to be worth $6bn in 2019, rising to $30bn by 2030, becoming an alternative asset class.

We've seen great traction:

- In the last year revenue has increased by 496% (YE 2022 Rev £1.7m, EBITDA -£208k)

- AOV for new customers is £318. £409 for repeat customers

- Blended Return on Ad Spend of 8.3x

- Launch pledge to remove plastic from the ocean for every online transaction

- Featured in GQ, The Times, Forbes

We’ll be using the funds to continue our growth, and finalise our pre-loved marketplace platform for worn sneakers to further drive the circular economy.
