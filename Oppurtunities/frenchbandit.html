Idea

In 2020, Mathieu adopted a Romanian dog living in the streets. When looking for some accessories to make his new friend as stylish as possible, he could only find products that were boring & nonfunctional. Named after his dog, Bandit was born.

Bandit’s mission is to make dogs & cats stylish by offering them accessories, without compromising quality or style. We’ve already released 30+ products and have even collaborated with Lancel.

Bandit has grown to 1.6M€ revenue (EBITDA -€716k) in 2021, selling 130k+ products to 55k+ happy pet-parents. Bandit is already loved by 70k+ followers across Facebook & Instagram. Currently 90% of our sales are D2C and we are developing strong partnerships with general stores such as Le Printemps Haussman, les Galeries Lafayette, Le Bon Marché.

The European pet market has grown 14% in the last 3 years and is now worth €5 billion.

In early 2022 we raised a seed round of 1.09M€ with business angels : Augustin Paluel-Marmont (Michel & Augustin), Patrick Asdaghi (Foodcheri), Adrien Ducousset (Wamiz), Yoann Latouche (YLG group).

Now we want to extend that round to our community. In the future we also aim to start using the funds to expand our product range and focus on high-end quality products for cats, increase the team and begin the development of a physical store presence.
