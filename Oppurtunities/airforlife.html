Idea

10-30% of the worlds population suffers from allergic rhinitis and over 300 million people worldwide suffer from asthma. Global pandemics also severely impact human health and are a threat to people’s lives. Today, the global air purification market is worth $12.26 billion and is forecasted to grow at 8.1% CAGR until 2030. Air For Life (AFL) is a UK Government supported Health Technology Company, specialising in Air Purification and Infection Control. We produce two flagship consumer air purification products, built with NASA developed technology called Sanifiers®. During the pandemic, demand for air purifiers and sanitising solutions soared and AFL experienced a significant increase in sales to £1.4m FY21, (EBITDA £133,800). We distribute our products to 15 countries and in Nov 2020, AFL formed a strategic partnership with a global telematics company called OCTO Telematics SpA, headquartered in Italy. Our products have been co-branded as a Mercedes Benz Accessory and we are in current discussions to be trialed in Pets at Home stores in the UK. We plan to use our funds for: Key hires: in-house specialist engineers, to help us expand our product range and research & development. Expand Inventory: by creating tooling in the UK, we can cut down lead time & further stabilise our supply chain. Marketing: to increase sales and grow our brand awareness.
