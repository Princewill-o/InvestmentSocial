Oppurtunities 


Air For Life

Air For Life is a health tech company using NASA technology to produce air purification products. They have had a revenue growth of 243% for 2018-2021 and co-branded products with Mercedes Benz. They are now raising funds to grow their brand awareness in the retail market.

TheEditLDN

The Edit LDN is a rapidly growing global online marketplace (seeing 496% revenue growth in the last year) aiming to disrupt the limited sneaker market. They connect trusted resellers to a highly engaged audience via a Premium Online Retail environment, and its Harrods concession.

 

Vidas Prime

VidasPrime is on a mission to become the largest on-demand private healthcare medical network in Spain: a marketplace for patients & private health professionals. With €1.3M+ revenue and -€472k EBITDA between 2019 & 2021, while in 2021 alone, it generated €274k in Revenue & €4.9k EBITDA. 8k+ patient enquiries & 150+ paying healthcare providers.

 

UpCircle 

Founded on sustainability and social responsibility, UpCircle elevate leftover natural ingredients, bringing them back to life as award-winning skincare products. They're natural, vegan, cruelty-free, palm oil-free, plastic negative, handmade and housed in recyclable and refillable packaging.

Kitepower

Kitepower systems generate electricity by flying kites. One 60m2 Kite will generate enough electricity to power 150 households while weighing only 60 kg and unlike solar PV it generates 24h a day. 15 years of research, 6 years of commercial development and over €4M invested in R&D.

YASAI

YASAI redesigns food systems by integrating Vertical Farming as an infrastructure based on circular economy. Developed in only 2 years, YASAI already sells its products to the #1 Swiss retailer. Their goal is to double production capacity to 40t p.a. to meet their prospective consumers’ increasing demand.

Wild Atlantic Distillery

Wild Atlantic are a family-owned distillery, part of the new wave of independent, traditional and craft distilleries in Ireland, reviving Irish Whiskey production in Derry after 100 years. With sales forecasted to double by 2030 in the US, Wild Atlantic now seeks funds to double the production of its Irish Whiskey.

EcoSync

To achieve net zero, the world needs to stop wasting energy. EcoSync’s smart tech enables commercial buildings to stop heating empty rooms, reducing energy consumption & costs by a possible 50%. With customer orders up 260% since the end of 2021 & clients incl. Oxford University, they are raising funds to accelerate growth.

TOG Knives

TOG Knives is a Bristol-based brand that designs & sells premium kitchen knives, batch-produced in the Samurai sword capital of Japan. Revenue has grown 4.9X since its 2019 Crowdcube raise and the company is profitable. Raising again to develop new products and packaging and drive further growth.

French Bandit

With 80k+ orders and 70k+ followers on social media, Bandit is a key player in the French pet accessories market. Revenue to date is 1,6M€+ and in the future, they want to serve more pet owners with the aim of starting to create new products and the development of a physical presence of stores.
